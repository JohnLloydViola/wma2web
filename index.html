OUR STORY

Preserving the Flavors
of Home

Filipino cuisine is more than food — it is memory, family, and the binding thread of a diaspora spread across the world. Every dish carries the warmth of someone's kitchen, the hands of a lola, the laughter of a Sunday salu-salo.

Lutong Pinoy was created to preserve these culinary traditions — to document the recipes that define us, and share them with the world with the pride and care they deserve.
